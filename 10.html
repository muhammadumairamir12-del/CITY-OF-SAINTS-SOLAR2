Solar Inverters: The Brain of Your System

The inverter is the most crucial part of a solar system, converting DC current into AC current. We offer various types of inverters perfect for every system type (On-Grid, Off-Grid, Hybrid), and they also include smart monitoring features.

  • **Growatt:** Industry leader for residential and commercial inverters.
  • **Solis:** High-efficiency and reliable string inverters.
  • **Huawei:** Smart PV solutions with AI integration.
  • **SolarEdge:** Optimized inverter solutions for maximum energy production.

Choose the best inverter according to your needs; our experts are available to assist you.

Lithium-Ion Batteries: Power Backup Solutions

For off-grid and hybrid solar systems, reliable battery storage is essential. We only provide the latest LiFePO4 (Lithium Iron Phosphate) batteries, which offer better cycle life, safety, and efficiency compared to older lead-acid batteries.

  • **High Cycle Life:** 6000+ discharge cycles.
  • **Integrated BMS:** Battery Management System for safety and optimal performance.
  • **Scalable Solutions:** Capacities from 5kWh to 20kWh, which can be expanded according to your needs.
  • **Long Lifespan:** Up to 10 years warranty.

Maintain continuous power supply even during load shedding.

General Solar Questions

**Q: What is solar energy?**
A: Solar energy is a method of generating electricity from sunlight. Solar panels absorb sunlight and convert it into electricity.

**Q: What types of solar systems are there?**
A: There are three main types: On-Grid (connected to the utility grid), Off-Grid (independent from the grid with battery backup), and Hybrid (a combination of both).

**Q: How can I get a zero electricity bill?**
A: With an On-Grid system, if your solar production exceeds your consumption, you can sell the extra electricity to the utility through "Net Metering" to reduce your bill to zero.

Installation & Maintenance

**Q: How long does solar system installation take?**
A: For residential homes, it usually takes 2-3 days. Commercial or industrial projects may take longer, depending on the project size.

**Q: How are solar panels cleaned?**
A: Panels should be regularly cleaned with water and a soft brush to maintain their efficiency. Our AMC service includes professional cleaning.

**Q: Will a solar system work during load shedding?**
A: On-Grid systems shut down during grid outages for safety reasons. However, Off-Grid and Hybrid systems provide electricity during load shedding with battery backup.

Cost & Savings

**Q: How much does it cost to install a solar system?**
A: The cost depends on the system size (kW), panel and inverter quality, and batteries. We offer a free site survey and a customized quote.

**Q: What warranty do solar systems come with?**
A: Our panels have a 25-year performance warranty, and inverters come with a 5-10 year product warranty. Our installations also include a 10-year service warranty.

**Q: Are installment plans available for solar systems?**
A: Yes, we offer easy installment plans in collaboration with our bank partners.
